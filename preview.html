What is Expected Goals (xG)?

Expected Goals measures the quality of a shot based on variables like assist type, shot angle, distance, and defender pressure.

Our proprietary model is trained on 11,830 shots, taking coordinate streams directly from live games to calculate optimal probability matrices.
