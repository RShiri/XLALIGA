New here? What is xG?

Expected goals (xG) rates every shot by how likely it was to be scored, from 0 to 1. A tap-in might be 0.7 xG; a hopeful 30-metre strike 0.03. Sum a team's shots and you get how many goals an average finisher would score from those chances — a better "who deserved it" number than the scoreline alone.

La Liga xG is layered in from the deep-scrape (Understat + WhoScored). Until a match is deep-scraped it contributes to the table but not to these charts.

Season Projection

Where does the table finish? A Poisson model built from this season's goals (home/away attack & defence strengths) simulates the remaining fixtures thousands of times to estimate final points and the odds of the title, a top-four/European finish, and relegation.
